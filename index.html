About the Gym

Eastside Boxing Club 517 LLC operates Statewide Fitness with a focus on youth development, fitness, and community support. Boxing is used as a foundation for building confidence, discipline, and healthy habits.

Meet the Coaches

Kolmarji Harris

Founder

Kolmarji Harris is a lifelong boxer, mentor, and community leader. He uses boxing as a tool to teach discipline, confidence, and resilience, with a strong focus on youth development and community support.

Andrew Beals

Head Coach

Andrew Beals is a USA Boxing–certified coach, nationally certified sports medicine personal trainer, and Marine veteran. He mentors youth and families through boxing, fitness, and life skills, building champions inside and outside the ring.
